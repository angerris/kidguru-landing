Kidguru an innovative platform designed to address the gap in parental knowledge about their children's health and development.

Our app
Our app is a digital assistant that provides parents with reliable and convenient tools for effective child care at all stages of their lives.
Our mission
Our mission is to raise the level of knowledge of parents, facilitate the work of doctors and create conditions for the long-term well-being of families and strengthen the health of the future generation
SOLUTIONS
Reference Book
  • Comprehensive health and development information.
  • Based on professional sources like WHO, AAP, etc.
  • Tracks development from birth to age 18.
Documents storage
  • Provides parents with constant access to their child’s health information.
  • Encryption of data accessible only to the account and its family.
  • Data storage in secure AWS cloud services.
  • TBD: certification for compliance with American and European standards.
Symptom calendar
  • Logs symptoms, doctor appointments, medications, and vaccinations.
  • Upload and share medical history and questionnaires with doctors.
Children’s Profile
  • A child profile enabling parents to share their child’s medical information with their pediatrician.
  • Upload just in two clicks.
